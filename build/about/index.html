Our mission

We are pursuing two parallel goals with the Codio platform. One is to provide a completely free platform that serves the needs of education, whether schools, universities or any organization interested in teaching people to learn how to code.

We are also building a platform that aims to be the very best web based IDE focused on front-end web development. As well as delivering an IDE that will evolve into a unique way of building everything from web sites to applications, libraries and frameworks, we are going to be adding plugins that cover more and more of the development lifecycle.

We will be releasing a range of exciting plugins such as

  • Web based documentation, user-guides and manuals
  • Wikis for managing your development projects
  • Bug tracking and issues management
  • Prezi style presentations
  • Diagramming

  • and more to be announced later this year
